Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our review process and partnerships.

Do you accept products for review?

Yes! We're always looking for innovative eco-friendly travel gear to test and review. Please contact us with product details, sustainability information, and company background.

How do you choose products?

We prioritize products based on their environmental impact, innovation, user demand, and alignment with our sustainability criteria. See our About page for details.

Do you offer consulting?

We occasionally work with companies on sustainability consulting projects. Contact us with details about your project and we'll let you know if it's a good fit.

Can I contribute?

We're always interested in hearing from passionate sustainable travel writers. Please send us samples of your work and your areas of expertise.
