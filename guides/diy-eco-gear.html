Creating your own travel gear is one of the most rewarding aspects of sustainable travel. Not only do you save money and reduce waste, but you also end up with unique, personalized accessories that perfectly suit your needs.

No-Sew Packing Cubes

üïê 30 minutes üåü Beginner üí∞ $0 (upcycled)

Materials

  • 2-3 large cotton t-shirts
  • Scissors
  • Measuring tape
  • Fabric marker

Benefits

  • Zero cost using old clothes
  • Soft and flexible
  • Machine washable
  • Fully customizable sizes

Steps

  1. Cut off sleeves and neckline to create a rectangle
  2. Cut 12" x 16" rectangle for bottom
  3. Cut 4" strips for sides
  4. Tie corners with simple knots
  5. Create drawstring from sleeve material
  6. Thread and secure drawstring

Homemade Solid Toiletries

üïê 2 hours üåü Intermediate üí∞ $15-25

Basic Recipe

  • 100g coconut oil
  • 50g cocoa butter
  • 25g castor oil
  • 75g SCI powder
  • Essential oils
  • Corn starch

Benefits

  • TSA-friendly
  • Zero plastic waste
  • Customizable ingredients
  • Cost-effective

Steps

  1. Melt oils and butters
  2. Add SCI powder gradually
  3. Mix to paste consistency
  4. Add remaining ingredients
  5. Pour into molds
  6. Let cure for 24-48 hours
